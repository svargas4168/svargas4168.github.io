DVPN vs. VPN

We'll explain the advantages and disadvantages.

disvantages of a VPN over a DVPN(decentralized virtual private network).

The servers used by regular VPNs are controlled entirely by the VPN provider. Therefore, while your activity is hidden from your ISP and the sites you visit, your VPN can see everything, as all of the servers are centralized and owned and operated by a single entity.
